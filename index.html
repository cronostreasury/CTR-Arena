How Scoring Works
Transparent, on-chain scoring that rewards real capital contribution.
💰

Buy Volume

3×

Every dollar of buy volume earns 3 points. Buying CTR grows the treasury.

📤

Sell Volume

1×

Sell volume earns 1 point. Natural but contributes less to growth.

🛡️

Anti-Wash

0–1×

If sells approach buys, score drops to zero. Only net buyers get rewards.

📐 The Scoring Formula

This system rewards real capital inflow. Repeated buying and selling without increasing your position reduces your score to zero.

// Step 1: Raw Points Raw Points = (3 × Buy Volume) + (1 × Sell Volume) // Step 2: Anti-Wash Factor Anti-Wash = max( min( (Buys − Sells) / Buys, 1 ), 0 ) // Step 3: Holding Bonus (max +50%) Holding Bonus = 1 + (Holding Duration / Season Length) × 0.5 // ═══ FINAL SCORE ═══ Final Score = Raw Points × Anti-Wash × Holding Bonus
