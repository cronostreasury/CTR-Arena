⚙️ How Scoring Works
A transparent system that rewards genuine capital contribution and penalizes wash trading.
💰

Buy Volume

3×

Every dollar of buy volume earns 3 points. Buying CTR grows the treasury and deserves maximum reward.

📤

Sell Volume

1×

Sell volume earns 1 point per dollar. Selling is natural but contributes less to treasury growth.

🛡️

Anti-Wash

0–1×

If your sells approach your buys, your score drops toward zero. Only net buyers get full rewards.

📐 The Formula

This system rewards real capital inflow. Repeated buying and selling without increasing your position will reduce your score. Only net buyers who contribute to treasury growth receive maximum rewards.

// Step 1: Raw Points
Raw Points = (3 × Buy Volume) + (1 × Sell Volume)

// Step 2: Anti-Wash Factor (penalizes equal buy/sell)
Anti-Wash = max( min( (Buys − Sells) / Buys, 1 ), 0 )

// Step 3: Holding Bonus (rewards diamond hands)
Holding Bonus = 1 + (Holding Duration / Season Length) × 0.5

// Final Score
Final Score = Raw Points × Anti-Wash Factor × Holding Bonus
✅ Buy = Sell → Score = 0
✅ Mostly buys → Score stays high
✅ Early holder → Up to +50% bonus
