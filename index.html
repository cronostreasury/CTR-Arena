How Scoring Works
Transparent on-chain scoring. Rewards real capital contribution.
💰

Buy Volume

3×

Every dollar of buy volume earns 3 points. Buying grows the treasury.

📤

Sell Volume

1×

Sell volume earns 1 point. Natural but less treasury impact.

🛡️

Anti-Wash

0–1×

Sells ≈ Buys → score → zero. Net buyers win.

📐 Scoring Formula

Repeated buy-sell without growing position = score → 0. Only real inflow earns max rewards.

// Step 1: Raw Points Raw Points = (3 × Buy Volume) + (1 × Sell Volume) // Step 2: Anti-Wash Factor Anti-Wash = max( min( (Buys − Sells) / Buys, 1 ), 0 ) // Step 3: Holding Bonus (max +50%) Holding Bonus = 1 + (Hold Duration / Season Length) × 0.5 // FINAL SCORE Final Score = Raw Points × Anti-Wash × Holding Bonus
